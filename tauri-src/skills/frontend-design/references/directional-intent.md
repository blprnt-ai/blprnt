# Directional Intent

## Definition
Directional intent is the single conceptual direction that guides every visual and interaction choice.

## Principles
- Pick a distinct direction and commit to it.
- Avoid blending multiple aesthetics without a clear hierarchy.
- Every choice must be a choice: justify the reason for each visual decision.

## Quick Checklist
- [ ] One sentence describing the direction.
- [ ] Three keywords that describe the experience.
- [ ] One non-negotiable visual signature (shape, color, or motion).
