# Subtle Layering

## Intent
Surfaces should be barely different yet clearly distinguishable.

## Techniques
- Use small deltas in background color or elevation.
- Limit to 2–3 surface tiers per view.
- Reinforce layers with borders or shadows, not both.

## Checks
- Adjacent surfaces are distinguishable at a glance.
- Layer transitions feel calm, not noisy.
- Hover/focus uses a slight lift rather than a new color set.
