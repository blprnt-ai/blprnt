# Typography Guidance

## Intent
Typography must be intentional, beautiful, and readable.

## Guidelines
- Choose fonts that are distinctive and still highly legible.
- Establish a clear scale (H1–H4, body, caption).
- Use consistent line heights and weights per tier.
- Avoid more than two font families per screen.

## Practical Checks
- Headings visually separate sections at a glance.
- Body text remains readable at 14–16px with 1.4–1.6 line height.
- Accent text is used sparingly for emphasis.
